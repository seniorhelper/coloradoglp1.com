Our story

The gap between a prescription
and real metabolic care.

Elevation Health was founded by Lindsay and Meredith — registered dietitians with over 30 years of combined clinical experience in nutrition, metabolic health, and peptide therapy.

They started Elevation Health to bridge a gap they kept watching people fall into: the space between prescriptive weight loss and true metabolic optimization. Most GLP-1 companies were shipping vials and disappearing. Most weight-loss programs were pushing willpower diets that never held. Nobody was doing both the medication and the plan.

So they built a model where every client gets the depth of care they deserve — the clinical protocol, the nutrition strategy, the human on the other end of a message when week three gets hard. GLP-1s and peptides alongside a real lifestyle plan, not as a replacement for one.

The focus isn’t just how you look. It’s how you perform, how you feel, and how you age. Our care is virtual, nationwide, and built around you.
